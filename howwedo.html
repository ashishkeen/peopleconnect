How do we do?

We listen. That's what we do.

• We want the person concerned, to talk

• We are non-judgemental

• We are unbiased

• We stay neutral to both families and individuals

• We maintain complete secrecy and confidentiality always via signed legal agreements

• We work in a neutral and serene place in some resort, amidst nature (but due to the pandemic, we will allow it to be virtual for a while)

• The interaction is therapeutic in many ways

• There is no medication prescribed

• A roadmap is shared at the end of the session

• The session might extend beyond 2N/2D depending on the severity and urgency of the individual concerned
